Why Berario

More than a bottle store. It's a cellar with a welcome.

i

A cellar, curated

Hundreds of wines and premium spirits, hand-picked from South Africa's best independent producers. If it's on our shelf, we've tasted it and we can tell you why.

ii

Tastings, properly hosted

Sit down with the winemakers themselves at our regular in-store tastings and pairing evenings. Bring your curiosity — we'll handle the rest.

iii

Good company, always

The neighbourhood spot where Northcliff comes to discover something new. Knowledgeable, never intimidating — and genuinely happy to see you.
